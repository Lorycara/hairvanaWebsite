How It Works

How HairMe Helps You Find Your Perfect Hairstyle

Discover the perfect look for your unique face shape in three simple steps

Analyze Your Face Shape

Take a selfie or upload a photo, and our AI will analyze your facial proportions to determine your face shape—oval, round, square, heart, or diamond—with precise accuracy.

Get Personalized Recommendations

Receive tailored hairstyle suggestions that complement your unique facial features. Our app considers your face shape, hair texture, and personal preferences to suggest styles that will truly enhance your appearance.

Review and Save Recommendations

Browse through your personalized hairstyle recommendations, save your favorites, and take them to your stylist. Get the perfect haircut with confidence knowing it's tailored to your unique features.
