Best Haircuts for Women: Top Styles for 2025

Looking for your next favorite hairstyle? Explore the top women's haircuts and trends, from chic bobs to flowing layers, and find inspiration.

Bob & Lob Haircuts

Classic Bob

A timeless cut hitting between the chin and shoulders. Versatile and chic, it suits many face shapes and hair textures.

Lob (Long Bob)

Grazing the shoulders or collarbone, the lob offers more length than a bob while remaining easy to manage and style.

Blunt Cut Bob/Lob

Features sharp, even ends for a modern, sophisticated look. Creates the appearance of thicker hair.

Layered Hairstyles

Long Layers

Ideal for adding movement and reducing weight in long hair. Layers can be customized to flatter any face shape.

Shag Haircut

Characterized by heavy layers around the crown and feathered ends. Offers a retro vibe with lots of texture.

Medium Length Layers

Layers add shape and volume to shoulder-length hair, preventing it from looking flat or heavy.

Short Haircuts

Pixie Cut

A bold, short style that can range from ultra-short to slightly longer on top. Highlights facial features.

Bixie Cut

A hybrid between a bob and a pixie. Offers the ease of short hair with slightly more length and styling versatility.

Short Bob

A bob cut shorter than chin-length, often hitting around the earlobe or jaw. Can be chic and edgy.

Hairstyles with Bangs

Curtain Bangs

Longer bangs parted in the middle, framing the face softly. Very popular and flattering for many face shapes.

Side-Swept Bangs

Bangs swept to one side of the forehead. A classic choice that can soften features and add dimension.

Wispy Bangs

Light, feathered bangs that offer a softer alternative to full, blunt bangs. Add a delicate touch to any style.
