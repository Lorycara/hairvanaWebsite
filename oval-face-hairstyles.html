Understanding Oval Face Shape

An oval face shape is characterized by balanced proportions with the forehead slightly wider than the chin and gentle curves at the sides. It's often considered the "ideal" face shape because it's naturally balanced and can support a wide variety of hairstyles.

With an oval face, you have the flexibility to experiment with different styles while maintaining a flattering look. Your face has natural balance, but the right hairstyle can enhance your best features even further.

Characteristics of Oval Face Shape

  • Forehead slightly wider than the chin
  • Balanced, harmonious facial proportions
  • Face length approximately 1.5 times the width
  • Gently curved jawline
  • No particularly dominant facial feature

Styling Tips for Oval Face Shape

  • Experiment freely: Oval faces can support virtually any hairstyle, so don't be afraid to try new looks
  • Consider hair texture: Choose styles that work with your natural hair texture for easy maintenance
  • Frame your features: Use face-framing layers to highlight your best features
  • Play with proportions: Your balanced face shape allows you to experiment with both volume and length
  • Try different parts: Middle, side, or deep side parts all work well with oval face shapes
  • Bangs flexibility: All bang styles complement oval faces—from blunt to side-swept to curtain bangs
