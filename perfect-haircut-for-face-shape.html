Find Your Hairstyle: Best Cuts by Face Shape

Oval Face

You have: Balanced proportions, forehead slightly wider than chin

Try these:

  • Medium-length cuts with layers
  • Side-parted styles
  • Pixie cuts (women)
  • Classic crew cuts (men)

Avoid: Heavy bangs, excessive volume

Round Face

You have: Similar width/length, full cheeks, rounded chin

Try these:

  • Volume at the top
  • Side-swept bangs
  • Long bobs with layers
  • Pompadour styles (men)

Avoid: One-length bobs, center parts

Square Face

You have: Strong jawline, straight sides, angular features

Try these:

  • Soft, layered cuts
  • Side-swept styles
  • Textured bobs
  • Volume at the crown

Avoid: Blunt cuts, center parts

Heart Face

You have: Wide forehead/cheekbones, narrow chin

Try these:

  • Chin-length or longer
  • Side-parted bangs
  • Width at jaw level
  • Layered chin-length bobs

Avoid: Top-heavy styles, blunt bangs

Diamond Face

You have: Narrow forehead, wide cheekbones, narrow chin

Try these:

  • Volume at forehead and chin
  • Side-swept bangs
  • Chin-length layered cuts
  • Forward-styled cuts

Avoid: Middle parts, slicked-back styles

Rectangle Face

You have: Longer face, similar width throughout

Try these:

  • Volume on the sides
  • Layered cuts with fullness
  • Bangs to shorten the face
  • Textured, disheveled looks

Avoid: Long straight styles, vertical volume

Frequently Asked Questions: How to Find Your Hairstyle

How do I know what hairstyle suits me?

To find what hairstyle suits you best, identify your face shape first, then consider your hair texture, maintenance preferences, and lifestyle. Your ideal hairstyle should complement your face shape while working with your natural hair type.

How can I find the perfect hairstyle for my face shape?

To find the perfect hairstyle for your face shape: 1) Identify your face shape using our guide above, 2) Browse hairstyles recommended for your specific shape, 3) Consider your hair texture and density, and 4) Use the HairMe app for personalized recommendations based on AI analysis of your unique features.

What hairstyle should I get based on my face shape?

The best hairstyle for your face shape varies: oval faces suit most styles; round faces benefit from styles with height and angles; square faces look great with soft layers; heart-shaped faces work well with chin-length cuts; and rectangular faces benefit from volume on the sides.
