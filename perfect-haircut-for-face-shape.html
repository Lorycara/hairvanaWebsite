How to Find Your Face Shape

1

Pull hair back from your face

2

Take a front-facing photo

3

Notice forehead, cheekbones, jawline

4

Match to face shapes below

Finding your true face shape can be tricky. Many people have a combination of shapes!

Best Haircuts by Face Shape

Oval Face

You have: Balanced proportions, forehead slightly wider than chin

Try these:

  • Medium-length cuts with layers
  • Side-parted styles
  • Pixie cuts (women)
  • Classic crew cuts (men)

Avoid: Heavy bangs, excessive volume

Round Face

You have: Similar width/length, full cheeks, rounded chin

Try these:

  • Volume at the top
  • Side-swept bangs
  • Long bobs with layers
  • Pompadour styles (men)

Avoid: One-length bobs, center parts

Square Face

You have: Strong jawline, straight sides, angular features

Try these:

  • Soft, layered cuts
  • Side-swept styles
  • Textured bobs
  • Volume at the crown

Avoid: Blunt cuts, center parts

Heart Face

You have: Wide forehead/cheekbones, narrow chin

Try these:

  • Chin-length or longer
  • Side-parted bangs
  • Width at jaw level
  • Layered chin-length bobs

Avoid: Top-heavy styles, blunt bangs

Diamond Face

You have: Narrow forehead, wide cheekbones, narrow chin

Try these:

  • Volume at forehead and chin
  • Side-swept bangs
  • Chin-length layered cuts
  • Forward-styled cuts

Avoid: Middle parts, slicked-back styles

Rectangle Face

You have: Longer face, similar width throughout

Try these:

  • Volume on the sides
  • Layered cuts with fullness
  • Bangs to shorten the face
  • Textured, disheveled looks

Avoid: Long straight styles, vertical volume
