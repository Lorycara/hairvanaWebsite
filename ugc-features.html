Features to Show

Here are the key HairMe features to showcase in your videos. Each feature includes tips on how to best present it.

1) AI Face Shape Analysis

This is the core feature of HairMe. The AI analyzes your face shape in seconds to determine your unique facial proportions and features. This is the first step users take when opening the app.

Tip: Show the analysis process in action - capture the moment when the AI scans the face and reveals the face shape result. This creates excitement and demonstrates the technology.

2) Personalized Hairstyle Recommendations

After analyzing the face shape, HairMe provides tailored hairstyle recommendations that match the user's unique features. This is where users discover their perfect look.

Tip: Show the variety of recommendations available. Scroll through different options to demonstrate the personalized nature of the suggestions.

3) Attractiveness Score

HairMe provides AI-powered beauty scores that analyze how different hairstyles impact your overall attractiveness. This helps users understand what works best for them.

Tip: Show the before and after scores when trying different hairstyles. This visual comparison is powerful and shows the value of choosing the right style.

4) Progress Tracking

Users can save their favorite looks, compare before and after photos, and track their hairstyle journey over time. This feature helps users see their transformation.

Tip: Show the progress timeline or before/after comparison feature. This demonstrates the long-term value and helps users see their improvement.

5) Hair Health Analysis

HairMe's advanced AI scans your hair condition, identifies problems, and provides detailed insights about your hair health. This helps users maintain healthy, strong hair.

Tip: Show the hair scan results screen. Highlight the detailed insights and recommendations provided, as this adds significant value beyond just hairstyle suggestions.

6) App Store Page

Although showing the app store page is not always required, it's a great way to visually show the first step to finding your perfect hairstyle, as it's the first thing users see after tapping the download button!

Tip: Show the app icon and name clearly. This creates brand recognition and makes it easy for viewers to find the app.
