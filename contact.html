Frequently Asked Questions

How do I determine my face shape?

To determine your face shape, pull your hair back and look straight into a mirror. Trace the outline of your face and examine its proportions. You can also measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline, as well as the length of your face from hairline to chin. Compare these measurements to our face shape guides for accurate identification.

Can I request a custom hairstyle recommendation?

Yes! While our guides provide general recommendations based on face shapes, we understand that individual preferences, hair texture, and lifestyle factors matter too. Fill out our contact form with details about your face shape, hair type, and preferences, and our experts will provide personalized recommendations.

Do you offer virtual consultations?

We currently don't offer virtual consultations, but we're working on implementing this feature in the future. Stay tuned by signing up for our newsletter to be notified when virtual consultations become available.

How often should I update my hairstyle?

This depends on personal preference, but most people benefit from refreshing their look every 6-12 months. However, your maintenance schedule should align with your hair's growth rate and the complexity of your style. Many clients find seasonal updates provide a nice balance between consistency and freshness.
