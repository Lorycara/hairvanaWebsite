Best Hairstyles for Square Face Shape

Find the perfect hairstyles to soften and complement your strong, angular features with these expert recommendations

Understanding Square Face Shape

A square face shape is characterized by a strong, angular jawline with roughly equal width at the forehead, cheekbones, and jaw. The sides of your face are straight, and your jawline has minimal curve, creating a bold, defined appearance.

With the right hairstyle, you can soften angular features while still showcasing the striking symmetry and strength of your square face shape.

Characteristics of Square Face Shape

  • Strong, angular jawline with minimal curve
  • Similar width at forehead, cheekbones, and jaw
  • Straight sides to the face
  • Wide hairline and defined forehead
  • Often symmetrical features and proportions

Styling Tips for Square Face Shape

  • Avoid blunt-cut bobs: Very straight, blunt cuts can accentuate the angularity of square faces
  • Create volume on top: Height at the crown helps elongate the face and balance strong jawlines
  • Try off-center parts: Side or off-center parts create asymmetry that softens square proportions
  • Add texture and layers: Layered cuts with texture soften the strong lines of square faces
  • Consider soft curls or waves: Adding gentle waves helps offset angular features
  • Try wispy bangs: Soft, wispy bangs can help counterbalance a strong jaw
