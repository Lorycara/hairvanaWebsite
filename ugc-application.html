Hey, Thank you for showing your interest in becoming a UGC creator for HairMe! We are a leading hairstyle app with AI-powered face shape analysis, helping people find their perfect look. We're looking for authentic creators to join our team!

Please carefully read through this document for instructions on how to apply. We need you to create 1 piece of content, following our guidelines and using previous content as inspiration.

What We're Looking For

UGC creators who can connect with our audience through genuine, relatable content. If you enjoy our product and think it will help people, we'd be happy to have you join with us!

Your videos should:

  • Feel personal and conversational (not overly polished or scripted)
  • Share real experiences, transformations, or testimonials
  • Highlight specific HairMe features that made a difference
  • Inspire action and confidence
  • Be between 15-60 seconds long

Content Guidelines

✅ Do:

  • Speak directly to camera in a quiet, well-lit space (if talking head)
  • Be authentic and genuine
  • Use natural language (avoid corporate speak)
  • Show your face when comfortable
  • Mention specific HairMe features
  • Keep energy positive but genuine

❌ Don't:

  • Make false or exaggerated claims
  • Use misleading before/after comparisons
  • Share others' personal information
  • Over-edit or use excessive effects
  • Make guarantees about specific results
  • Use copyrighted music without permission

(Important) Resources

We've provided comprehensive resources to help you create amazing content. Please review all of these before creating your submission:

  • Example hooks and structures
  • Features to show
  • User Pain-Points And Dream Outcomes
  • High-performing Content

What to Submit

After viewing the user pain points and dream outcomes & reviewing the high-performing content, we want you to create a 15-60 second video ad for HairMe that you believe would perform well on all platforms (TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts). Use the examples above as inspiration, but make it your own.

Your submission should include:

  • A strong hook in the first 3-5 seconds
  • Authentic storytelling or testimonial
  • At least one mention of HairMe within the first 10 seconds
  • A clear call-to-action

We need UGC creators that have agency, can think for themselves and care about our brand/product.

We have provided a lot of resources for you in here, and we expect you to use your initiative in creating your creative!

We look forward to seeing your submission!
