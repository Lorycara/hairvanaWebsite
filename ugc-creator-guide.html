Best Practices

Hook (First 3-5 seconds)

  • Start with a question or bold statement
  • Address a pain point immediately
  • Create curiosity or urgency
  • Examples: "I finally found my perfect hairstyle" or "This app changed how I look"

Mention HairMe Early

Always mention HairMe within the first 10 seconds of your video. This is crucial for brand recognition and platform algorithms.

Call-to-Action

End every video with a clear call-to-action. Examples:

  • "Download HairMe in the App Store"
  • "Link in bio to try it yourself"
  • "Get your perfect hairstyle with HairMe"

How to Show the App

The main features to show are the hairstyle recommendation and the hair health scan results.

Hairstyle Recommendation

Display the personalized hairstyle suggestions tailored to the user's face shape and features.

Hair Health Scan Results

Show the hair health analysis results with detailed insights about hair condition and recommendations.

How to Record

You can show yourself taking the photos for the scan, record yourself making a scan, or, if your video doesn't require it, record only the scan result (face shape analysis results).
